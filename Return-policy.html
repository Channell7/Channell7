RETURN POLICY

1. Returns Overview

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, you can return most items within 30 days of receiving your order for a full refund or exchange, as long as they meet the conditions outlined below.

2. Eligibility for Returns

  • Items must be returned within 30 days of delivery.
  • The product must be unused, in original condition, and in its original packaging.
  • Proof of purchase (receipt, order confirmation, etc.) must be included in your return package.
  • Any promotional or discounted items may be subject to different return conditions. Please refer to the offer details for return instructions.

3. Non-Returnable Items

  • Gift cards or vouchers are non-refundable.
  • Items that are final sale or marked as "non-returnable" cannot be returned.
  • Customized or personalized items are not eligible for return unless damaged or defective.

4. How to Return an Item

To initiate a return, follow these steps:

  • Contact our Customer Support team at support@shoeflip.com or visit our "Contact Us" page to request a Return Authorization (RA) number.
  • Package the items securely in their original packaging. Include the RA number and your proof of purchase.
  • Ship the items back to us using a trackable shipping service. You are responsible for the return shipping costs unless the return is due to a mistake on our part (e.g., wrong item sent).

5. Refunds

Once we receive your returned item and confirm it meets the return criteria, we will process your refund or exchange. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method. Please note that it may take several days for the refund to appear on your account, depending on your payment provider.

6. Exchanges

If you would like to exchange an item for a different size, color, or style, please follow the same return process and indicate the desired exchange item. If the requested exchange item is out of stock, we will issue a refund to your original payment method.

7. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us within 7 days of receiving your order. We will gladly assist you with a replacement or full refund. You may be asked to provide pictures of the damaged item and packaging to help us process your claim.

8. Shipping Costs

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer, except in cases where the return is due to an error on our part (e.g., wrong or defective item). We recommend using a trackable shipping service to ensure your return is delivered safely.
